Background

Why I do this

Marketing, to me, is the art of making the right people feel something — and then giving them a reason to act. I came into it from engineering, which means I think in systems, measure everything, and always ask "why did that work?"

At TheAgence, I ran AI-powered campaigns for Glow & Lovely and CloseUp — real brands with real briefs and real deadlines. Before that, I spent two years building a donor acquisition pipeline from scratch at an IIT Kharagpur NGO, learning what actually makes people say yes.

I'm fluent in the new tools — SEO, GEO, social media, Apollo.io, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, and a full suite of AI content tools. But what I bring beyond the tools is a growth mindset: the belief that every campaign is an experiment, and every experiment teaches you something.
